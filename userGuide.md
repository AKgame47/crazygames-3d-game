# MAD RUN: Rise Through Insanity - User Guide

## Website Overview

**Purpose:** An ultra-addictive 3D single-player action survival game where you run, fight, and upgrade through five insane difficulty modes to become "THE MAD ONE."

**Access:** Public access - no login required to play (optional login for leaderboards and progress saving)

---

## Powered by Manus

This game is built with cutting-edge technology:

- **Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript + Three.js 3D Engine + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Express.js + tRPC + MySQL Database
- **Audio:** Tone.js for procedural music + Howler.js for sound effects
- **Physics:** Three.js with Rapier physics engine
- **Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN and real-time leaderboards

## Using Your Game

### Getting Started

1. **Launch the Game:** Click "Start Game" on the main menu to begin your journey
2. **Select Your Mode:** Choose from five difficulty levels:
   - **Noob Mode** - Perfect for learning the basics with weak enemies
   - **Pro Mode** - Unlock after surviving 3 waves in Noob Mode
   - **God Mode** - Face boss fights and powerful abilities
   - **Psycho Mode** - Gravity flips and chaos await
   - **Ultimate Mode** - Endless waves for the truly mad

### Core Gameplay

**Controls:**
- **WASD** - Move your character in all directions
- **SPACE** - Jump to dodge incoming attacks
- **CLICK** - Attack enemies with your equipped weapon
- **SHIFT** - Activate special skills (when unlocked)

**Objective:** Survive waves of increasingly difficult enemies, defeat them to earn XP and coins, and progress through modes by unlocking new abilities and skills.

### Progression System

Each mode rewards you with:
- **XP** - Earn experience points for every enemy defeated
- **Coins** - Currency to purchase upgrades and cosmetics
- **Skill Points** - Unlock new abilities like "Speed Boost," "Double Jump," and "Energy Slash"
- **High Scores** - Compete on the leaderboard for each mode

### Key Features

**Neon Cyber World:** Battle in a stunning cyberpunk environment with glowing grids, dynamic lighting, and immersive 3D combat

**Addictive Sound Design:** Procedurally generated music that adapts to your difficulty level, combined with satisfying attack and hit sound effects

**Wave-Based Combat:** Each mode features increasingly difficult enemy waves. Survive longer to unlock new content and achievements

**Skill Unlocks:** Master new abilities as you progress:
- Speed Boost (Noob Mode)
- Double Jump (Pro Mode)
- Energy Slash (God Mode)
- Time Freeze (Psycho Mode)
- Shadow Clone (Ultimate Mode)

## Managing Your Game

### Leaderboards

After each game session, your high score is automatically saved to the leaderboard for that mode. Check your ranking against other players and strive to become "THE MAD ONE."

### Progress Tracking

Your game progress is automatically saved including:
- High scores for each difficulty mode
- Total kills and playtime
- Unlocked abilities and costumes
- Achievements earned

### Settings

Access game settings to adjust:
- Master volume
- Music volume
- Sound effects volume
- Graphics quality (coming soon)

## Tips for Success

1. **Learn the Patterns:** Each enemy type has predictable attack patterns - learn them to dodge effectively
2. **Use Your Skills Wisely:** Skills have cooldowns - use them strategically when surrounded
3. **Stay Mobile:** Keep moving to avoid enemy attacks and trap damage
4. **Watch Your Health:** The red health bar above your character shows your remaining HP
5. **Aim for Combos:** Chain attacks together for bonus XP multipliers

- **Wave Master** - Survive 10+ waves in any mode
- **Speed Demon** - Complete a mode without taking damage
- **Skill Master** - Unlock all abilities
- **Leaderboard Legend** - Reach #1 on any mode leaderboard
